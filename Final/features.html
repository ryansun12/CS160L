Prorgam 4
11/23/2019

This program adds three new features while maintaining all the previous ones.
1. Zooming in and out with the scroll wheel when nothing (sphere or trees) is selected. This adjusts the setPerspective FOV value.
2. Camera panning when nothing is selected. Click on any blank part of the canvas and drag in the direction you want to pan to.
3. Camera movement in view direction. Only works in perspective view for obvious reasons. Click the middle mouse button AND scroll up or down to move the camera in the positive or negative view direction.
