This lab allows the user to draw red circles or blue squares onto the canvas depending on whether it was a left or right click.
Left click anywhere on the canvas to draw a red circle with radius 10 or right click to draw a blue square with side length 10.
Drawing at the same point will result in the newer shape appearing over the old one.
